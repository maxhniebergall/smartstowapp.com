How to Inventory Winter Tires with SmartStow

December 20, 2025 • 4 min read

To inventory your winter tires with SmartStow, use a voice command like, "Garage Tire Rack, Set of 4 Bridgestone Blizzaks, 7mm tread remaining." SmartStow lets you quickly record the location and details of your tires, making it easy to find them when winter rolls around.

Winter tires are essential for safe driving in snowy and icy conditions, but storing and keeping track of them during the off-season can be a hassle. Remembering where you stored them and the condition they're in becomes a yearly challenge. Traditional methods like spreadsheets or notes apps can be cumbersome and inefficient, especially when you're dealing with multiple sets of tires or limited storage space.

SmartStow offers a streamlined, voice-first solution to this problem. Instead of typing or writing, you simply speak the details of your stored tires, and SmartStow organizes everything for you. This guide will walk you through the process of inventorying your winter tires using SmartStow, ensuring you can quickly locate them when you need them.
