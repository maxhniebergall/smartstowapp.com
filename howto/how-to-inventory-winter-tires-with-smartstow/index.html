The Quick Voice Command Method for Winter Tires

SmartStow's voice command feature is the fastest way to inventory your winter tires. Here's a step-by-step guide:
  1. Open SmartStow: Launch the SmartStow app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Correct Location: If you've already set up your garage and tire rack locations, navigate to them within the app. If not, create these locations first.
  3. Initiate Voice Recording: Tap the voice recording button.
  4. Speak Your Command: Clearly state the location, a description of the tires, and any relevant details. For example: "Garage Tire Rack, Set of 4 Michelin X-Ice, 8mm tread depth."
  5. Confirm and Save: Review the transcribed text to ensure accuracy. Make any necessary corrections and save the entry.
With these simple steps, your winter tires are now inventoried and easily searchable within SmartStow. No more guessing where you put them or what condition they're in!
