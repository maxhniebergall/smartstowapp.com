How Do I Inventory a Watercolor Set with SmartStow?

February 10, 2026 • 5 min read

To inventory a watercolor set, ensure you are online, open SmartStow, and speak the details clearly. State the location, brand, and item description, such as "Art Box, Winsor & Newton 24-pan professional watercolor set," to instantly log your art supplies into your digital home inventory.

Managing a growing collection of art supplies can be a daunting task for any hobbyist or professional artist. Watercolor sets, in particular, often consist of numerous small pans, tubes, and specialized palettes that are easily misplaced or forgotten. Traditional methods of tracking these items, like writing in a notebook or typing into a spreadsheet, are often too slow and cumbersome to maintain during a creative session. This is where a voice-first approach becomes invaluable.

SmartStow offers a streamlined way to organize your studio by allowing you to record your inventory using only your voice. By speaking the details of your watercolor sets, you can create a comprehensive digital catalog without ever having to stop and type. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice commands to log your Winsor & Newton sets and other art essentials, ensuring you always know exactly what you have in stock and where it is located.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Art Supplies

The most efficient way to build your inventory is by using the voice logging feature. This method allows you to capture the specifics of your watercolor sets in seconds. Follow these steps to record your items:

  • Ensure your device has an active internet connection, as being online is required to record and add new items to your SmartStow database.
  • Open the SmartStow app and navigate to the recording interface.
  • Clearly state the storage location first, followed by the item name and specific details. For example, say: "Art Box, Winsor & Newton 24-pan professional watercolor set."
  • Wait for the app to process your voice recording and convert it into a text entry.
  • Review the generated entry to ensure the brand name and pan count were captured accurately.
  • Confirm the save to add the item to your permanent inventory list.

By recording by voice, you eliminate the need for manual data entry, making it much more likely that you will keep your inventory up to date as you acquire new colors or sets.
