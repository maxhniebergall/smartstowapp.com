How to Inventory a Circular Saw with Voice Commands?

January 21, 2026 • 4 min read

To inventory a circular saw, open SmartStow while online and speak the location and item details, such as 'Garage Tool Bench, Makita 7-1/4 inch corded circular saw.' The app uses voice recording to create a searchable text entry you can access and search even while offline.

Managing a growing collection of power tools can be a daunting task, especially when your garage or workshop becomes cluttered. A circular saw is a foundational tool for any DIY enthusiast or professional, but keeping track of its specific model, blade size, and storage location is essential for maintaining an efficient workspace. Traditional methods of inventory often fail because they require too much manual effort in environments where your hands are likely busy or dirty.

SmartStow offers a modern, voice-first solution to this problem. By focusing on voice logging rather than manual typing, you can document your entire tool collection in a fraction of the time. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice commands to ensure your circular saw is properly logged, categorized, and always easy to find, even when you are working in areas with poor internet connectivity.

The Quick Voice Command Method

The most efficient way to add a circular saw to your digital inventory is through direct voice recording. This method eliminates the need for typing on a screen, which is ideal when you are in the middle of a project. Follow these steps to ensure a successful entry:

  • Check Connectivity: Ensure your mobile device is connected to the internet before you begin recording, as the voice-to-text processing requires an active connection.
  • Open the App: Launch SmartStow and navigate to the 'Add Item' section.
  • Initiate Recording: Tap the microphone icon to start the voice logging process.
  • Speak Clearly: State the location first, followed by the item description. For example: "Garage Tool Bench, Makita 7-1/4 inch corded circular saw."
  • Review the Text: Once you stop speaking, the app will convert your voice recording into a text entry. Quickly verify that the brand and location were captured correctly.
  • Save the Entry: Confirm the details to add the tool to your permanent inventory list.
