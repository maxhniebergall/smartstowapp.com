How to Inventory a Pipe Wrench with Voice Commands?

January 31, 2026 • 5 min read

To inventory a pipe wrench, open SmartStow while online, navigate to your location, and speak the details clearly. For example, say 'Utility Shelf, 14-inch Ridgid heavy duty pipe wrench' to record the brand, size, and condition instantly without needing to type or use manual entry methods.

Managing a collection of heavy-duty hand tools can be a logistical challenge, especially when they are stored in specialized areas like a basement plumbing workshop. A pipe wrench is a vital tool for any homeowner, but because they come in various sizes and materials—such as cast iron or aluminum—keeping track of exactly which one you have on hand is essential for planning repairs. Traditional methods of inventory often fail because they require you to stop what you are doing, dry your hands, and type into a spreadsheet or write on a notepad.

SmartStow revolutionizes this process by focusing on a voice-first interface. Instead of struggling with manual data entry, you can simply speak the description of your tools as you organize them. This approach is particularly useful in basement environments where you might be handling greasy or dusty equipment. By using voice logging, you ensure that your inventory is accurate and detailed without the friction of traditional apps.

In this guide, we will walk through the specific steps to document your pipe wrenches and other plumbing tools. We will focus on the voice-to-text capabilities of SmartStow, ensuring you can build a robust digital catalog of your hand tools by simply speaking the details out loud.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Pipe Wrenches

Recording your tools should be as fast as using them. SmartStow allows you to log items by speaking naturally. Follow these steps to record your pipe wrench:

  • Ensure Connectivity: Make sure your device is connected to the internet via Wi-Fi or cellular data, as voice recording requires an active connection to process your speech.
  • Open the App: Launch SmartStow and navigate to the 'Basement' area, then select the 'Plumbing' sub-location.
  • Initiate Voice Logging: Tap the microphone icon to begin your voice recording session.
  • Speak Clearly: State the specific shelf or bin followed by the tool description. For example: "Utility Shelf, 14-inch Ridgid heavy duty pipe wrench."
  • Confirm the Entry: Review the text generated by your voice command and save the item to your digital inventory.

This method eliminates the need for typing, allowing you to move through your toolbox quickly. By recording by voice, you can inventory an entire plumbing rack in minutes rather than hours.
