How to Organize Your RC Car Collection with Voice Commands?

February 25, 2026 • 3 min read

To organize your RC car collection, use SmartStow to record item details by speaking into your device. Simply state the location and the specific model, such as 'Workbench, Traxxas Slash 4x4 VXL,' to instantly log your outdoor hobby gear into a searchable digital inventory.

Managing a growing collection of RC cars and outdoor hobby gear can quickly become overwhelming. Between various chassis types, spare parts, and specific battery requirements, keeping track of where everything is stored—and what condition it is in—is essential for any serious enthusiast. Traditional methods often fall short because they require stopping your work to type or write down details, which breaks your flow in the garage.

SmartStow offers a streamlined, voice-first approach to home inventory that eliminates the need for manual typing. By using voice recording to document your belongings, you can keep your hands free for repairs while ensuring every piece of equipment is accounted for. This guide will walk you through the process of logging a high-performance RC truck and managing your hobby space efficiently.

The Quick Voice Command Method for RC Inventory

The most efficient way to add a new item to your collection is by speaking the details directly into the app. This ensures that you capture the information the moment you place the item on the shelf.

  • Ensure Connectivity: Before adding a new item, make sure your device is online to process the voice-to-text transcription.
  • Open the Recording Interface: Launch SmartStow and activate the voice logging feature.
  • State the Location First: Begin your command with the specific area, such as 'Garage' or 'Workbench.'
  • Identify the Item: Clearly state the model name. For example: 'Workbench, Traxxas Slash 4x4 VXL short course truck.'
  • Review the Log: The app will transcribe your voice recording into a text entry. Confirm that the details are correct before saving.
  • Categorize Automatically: SmartStow will place the item in your 'Hobby: Outdoor' category based on your description.
