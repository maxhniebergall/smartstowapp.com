How to Inventory a Heat Gun with Voice Commands?

January 26, 2026 • 4 min read

To inventory a heat gun, open SmartStow while online and speak the location and item details clearly. For example, say "Garage Electrical, Wagner Furno 500 dual temperature heat gun." The app records your voice description, allowing you to search and view your tool collection even when offline.

Managing a growing collection of power tools can be a logistical challenge, especially in a busy garage environment. Specialized equipment like heat guns are often tucked away in cabinets or drawers, making them difficult to locate when you are in the middle of a project. Traditional methods of tracking these items often fail because they require too much manual effort or constant data connectivity.

SmartStow provides a modern solution by utilizing voice-first technology to streamline the inventory process. By speaking the details of your tools directly into the app, you eliminate the need for tedious typing or manual logging. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice recording to document your heat gun, ensuring you always know exactly where it is stored and what condition it is in.

Step-by-Step Voice Logging for Your Heat Gun

Recording your power tools should be a seamless part of your workflow. Follow these steps to log your heat gun using voice commands:

  • Ensure your mobile device has an active internet connection to process the voice-to-text recording.
  • Open the SmartStow app and navigate to the 'Add Item' section.
  • Tap the microphone icon to initiate the voice recording session.
  • Speak the location and item details clearly: "Garage Electrical, Wagner Furno 500 dual temperature heat gun."
  • Wait for the app to transcribe your spoken words into a text-based inventory entry.
  • Review the generated description to ensure accuracy and save the item to your digital catalog.
