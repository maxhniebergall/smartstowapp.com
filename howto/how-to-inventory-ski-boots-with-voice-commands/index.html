How to Inventory Ski Boots with Voice Commands?

January 16, 2026 • 4 min read

To inventory ski boots, open SmartStow while online and speak the location and details, such as "Hallway Closet, Salomon size 27.5, blue and black." The app records your voice description, allowing you to search and view your seasonal gear later, even when you are offline.

Managing seasonal gear like ski boots can be a logistical challenge, especially when items are tucked away in deep storage for most of the year. Traditional methods of tracking equipment often fail because they require too much manual effort or physical documentation that gets lost. Whether you are a weekend warrior or a professional instructor, knowing exactly where your boots are and what condition they are in is essential for a smooth start to the winter season.

SmartStow provides a modern, voice-first solution to this problem. By eliminating the need for manual typing or complex data entry, you can catalog your entire gear collection simply by speaking. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice recording to log your ski boots, ensuring they are easy to find when the first snow falls.

The Quick Voice Command Method

Recording your ski boots in SmartStow is designed to be a hands-free experience. Follow these steps to log your gear efficiently:

  • Ensure Connectivity: Before starting, make sure your device is connected to the internet, as recording new voice entries requires an active connection.
  • Open SmartStow: Launch the application on your mobile device.
  • Initiate Voice Logging: Tap the voice recording button to begin speaking the details of your item.
  • Speak the Location First: Start by stating where the item is being placed, such as "Closet, Gear Box."
  • Describe the Item: Clearly state the brand, model, and specific identifiers. For example: "Salomon size 27.5, blue and black."
  • Confirm the Entry: Once you stop speaking, SmartStow processes the voice-to-text data and saves the item to your digital inventory.
  • Verify the Placement: Check that the item has been correctly categorized under 'Seasonal Gear' for easy filtering later.
