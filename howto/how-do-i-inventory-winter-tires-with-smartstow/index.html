How Do I Inventory Winter Tires with SmartStow?

January 08, 2026 • 5 min read

To inventory winter tires, ensure you are online, open SmartStow, and record a voice command stating the location, brand, quantity, and condition. For example: 'Garage Shelf 4, Set of 4 Michelin Tires, 6mm tread.' Your tires are then searchable offline whenever you need to check their status.

Managing seasonal gear like winter tires can be a logistical challenge, especially when they are tucked away in the back of a garage or high on a tire rack. Traditional methods of tracking these items often involve messy spreadsheets or handwritten notes that are easily lost or difficult to update. SmartStow offers a modern, voice-first solution that allows you to catalog your automotive equipment in seconds without ever needing to type a single word. By using voice recording, you can maintain an accurate record of your tires' condition and location, ensuring you are prepared for the first snowfall of the year. This guide will walk you through the process of using SmartStow to manage your seasonal inventory efficiently. We will cover the specific voice commands needed to log your tires, the essential details you should include in every recording, and how to retrieve this information even when you are deep in a garage without a cellular signal.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Tire Inventory

The most efficient way to add winter tires to your digital inventory is through the SmartStow voice recording feature. Because this process uses advanced voice-to-text technology, you must have an active internet connection at the moment you record the entry. This ensures the highest level of accuracy for your item descriptions.

  • Step 1: Open the SmartStow app while connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the 'Add Item' section and tap the microphone icon to start your voice recording.
  • Step 3: Speak clearly and naturally. Start with the location, then the item name, and finally the specific details. For example: 'Garage Shelf 4, Set of 4 Michelin Tires, 6mm tread.'
  • Step 4: Wait a moment for the system to process your speech into a text entry.
  • Step 5: Confirm the details and save the item to your 'Seasonal Gear' category.

By speaking the details rather than typing them, you avoid the hassle of handling your phone with dirty hands after moving heavy tires. This hands-free approach is specifically designed for the rugged environment of a garage or storage unit.
