How to Inventory an Easel with Voice Commands?

February 12, 2026 • 5 min read

To inventory an easel, open SmartStow while online and speak the location and item details clearly. For example, say "Spare Room Art, Winsor & Newton Eden Tabletop Studio Easel." The app records your voice description, allowing you to search and view your art supplies offline later.

Managing a growing collection of art supplies can be a daunting task for any hobbyist or professional artist. Easels, in particular, are bulky items that often get tucked away in closets or spare rooms when not in use. Keeping track of which easel is where—and what condition it is in—is essential for maintaining an organized studio and ensuring you have the right equipment for your next project. Traditional methods of inventory management often fall short because they require manual data entry that interrupts the creative flow.

SmartStow offers a revolutionary approach to home inventory by focusing on a voice-first experience. Instead of spending hours typing into a spreadsheet or trying to organize physical notes, you can simply speak the details of your art equipment into the app. This method is particularly beneficial for artists whose hands may be covered in charcoal or paint, as it allows for hands-free logging of items as they are put away. By using voice recording, you create a digital record that is both detailed and easy to manage.

This guide will walk you through the process of using SmartStow to inventory your easels and other art supplies. We will cover the specific voice commands needed, the details you should include in your recordings, and how to utilize the offline search functionality to find your items whenever inspiration strikes.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Art Supplies

Recording your easel in SmartStow is a streamlined process that relies entirely on your voice. Because the app is designed for speed, you can log an item in seconds. Follow these steps to ensure your easel is recorded correctly:

  • Check Your Connection: Ensure your device is connected to the internet before you begin recording. SmartStow requires an online connection to process voice-to-text data and save new entries to your cloud-based inventory.
  • Open the App: Launch SmartStow on your mobile device.
  • Initiate Voice Logging: Tap the voice recording button to start the capture process.
  • Speak the Location First: Clearly state the room and sub-location. For example, say "Spare Room Art." This helps the app categorize the item immediately.
  • Describe the Item: Follow the location with the specific name of the item. A perfect command would be: "Spare Room Art, Winsor & Newton Eden Tabletop Studio Easel."
  • Confirm and Save: Once you stop speaking, the app will process the recording. Review the text to ensure it accurately reflects your voice command and save the entry.

By following this sequence, you ensure that your hobby equipment is filed under the correct category, making it much easier to manage your studio space.
