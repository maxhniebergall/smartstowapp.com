How to Inventory a Hockey Stick with Voice Commands?

March 06, 2026 • 4 min read

To inventory a hockey stick, open SmartStow while online and speak the item details—including brand, flex, and curve—directly into the app. Your voice recording is instantly converted to text, allowing you to organize your winter sports equipment in the garage without manual typing or external hardware.

Managing a collection of winter sports equipment can be a logistical challenge, especially when dealing with specific technical specifications like hockey stick flex and blade patterns. Traditional methods of tracking gear often fall short because they require manual data entry that is cumbersome when you are in the middle of organizing a garage or packing for a game. SmartStow solves this by providing a voice-first interface that allows you to log your inventory simply by speaking the details out loud.

By utilizing voice recording, you can maintain an accurate record of your CCM, Bauer, or Warrior sticks without ever needing to put down your gear to type on a screen. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice commands to document your hockey sticks, ensuring that your inventory is always up to date and accessible, even when you are offline at the ice rink.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Hockey Gear

The most efficient way to document your winter sports equipment is through the SmartStow voice logging system. Follow these steps to record your hockey stick details:

  • Ensure Connectivity: Make sure your device is connected to the internet, as an online connection is required to record and add new items to your inventory.
  • Activate Recording: Open the app and tap the voice recording button to begin capturing your item details.
  • Speak Clearly: State the location first, followed by the specific model and technical specs. For example, say: "Rack 1, CCM Ribcor Trigger 7 Pro, 85 flex, P29 curve."
  • Review the Text: The app will automatically convert your spoken words into a text entry. Verify that the brand and flex ratings were captured accurately.
  • Save to Category: Confirm the item is saved under the 'Sports: Winter' category for easy filtering later.
