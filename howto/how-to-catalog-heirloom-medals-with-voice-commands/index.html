How to Catalog Heirloom Medals with Voice Commands?

March 17, 2026 • 5 min read

To catalog heirloom medals, use SmartStow to record a voice description including the medal name, recipient, and location. Simply speak the details into the app while online to create a permanent digital record that you can search and view even when you are offline.

Heirloom medals represent more than just military or civic achievement; they are tangible links to family history and personal legacy. However, these small items are easily misplaced or forgotten in the back of closets and drawers. Traditional methods of tracking these valuables often fail because they require tedious manual entry or physical logs that are difficult to update. SmartStow offers a modern solution by allowing you to document your collection using only your voice.

By utilizing a voice-first approach, you can capture the rich history of each medal without the friction of typing on a small screen. This guide will walk you through the process of using SmartStow to create a comprehensive inventory of your medals, specifically focusing on items stored in areas like a study or memorabilia room. Whether you are managing a single shadow box or an extensive collection of service crosses, voice logging ensures every detail is preserved for future generations.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Medals

Recording your heirlooms should be as natural as telling the story behind them. SmartStow is designed to capture information through voice recording, making the process significantly faster than manual data entry. Follow these steps to log your medals:

  • Ensure Connectivity: Before you begin, make sure your device is connected to the internet. While SmartStow allows for offline searching, adding new items requires an active connection to process your voice recording.
  • Open the App: Launch SmartStow and navigate to the 'Add Item' section.
  • Speak Clearly: Use a descriptive command. For example, say: "Shadow Box, WWII Distinguished Service Cross, Grandpa."
  • Assign the Location: Once the voice recording is processed, assign the item to its specific location. In this case, select 'Study' and then the sub-category 'Memorabilia'.
  • Confirm the Entry: Review the text generated from your voice logging and save the entry to your digital inventory.

This method eliminates the need for complex menus. By simply speaking the details out loud, you create a searchable record that includes the item name, its historical context, and its exact physical location within your home.
