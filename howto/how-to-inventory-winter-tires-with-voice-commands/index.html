The Quick Voice Command Method for Tire Inventory

Recording your winter tires in SmartStow is designed to be a hands-free experience that fits naturally into your workflow while you are organizing your garage. Follow these steps to ensure a perfect record every time:

  • Check Your Connection: Before you begin recording, ensure your mobile device has an active internet connection. While searching is available offline, the initial voice-to-text processing requires you to be online.
  • Open the SmartStow App: Launch the application and navigate to the add item section where the voice recording interface is located.
  • Activate the Voice Recorder: Tap the recording icon and wait for the prompt to begin speaking.
  • Speak Your Details Clearly: Use a natural speaking voice to describe the item. For example, say: "Garage Shelf 4, Set of 4 Michelin Tires, 6mm tread."
  • Review the Transcription: SmartStow will instantly convert your speech into a text record. Quickly glance at the screen to ensure the brand and measurements were captured correctly.
  • Save to Category: Assign the record to the 'Seasonal Gear' category and set the location to 'Garage > Tire Rack' for easy filtering later.
  • Confirm the Entry: Once you hit save, the data is stored in your digital inventory and synced for future offline access.

This method eliminates the need to wipe your hands or remove work gloves just to update your inventory, making it the most efficient way to track bulky automotive gear.
