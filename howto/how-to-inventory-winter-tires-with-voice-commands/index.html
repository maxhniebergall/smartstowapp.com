How to Inventory Winter Tires with Voice Commands?

January 04, 2026 • 4 min read

To inventory winter tires, ensure you are online, open SmartStow, and record a voice description including the location, brand, quantity, and tread depth. For example: "Garage Shelf 4, Set of 4 Michelin Tires, 6mm tread." This creates a searchable record accessible even when you are offline.

Managing seasonal gear like winter tires can be a logistical challenge. These items are heavy, bulky, and often stored in out-of-the-way places like high garage shelves or deep storage racks. Traditional inventory methods often fail because they require you to stop what you are doing, wipe your hands, and manually type data into a phone or write on a clipboard. This friction leads to disorganized storage and forgotten maintenance details.

SmartStow solves this problem by providing a voice-first interface designed for high-utility environments. By using voice recording instead of manual data entry, you can document your tires the moment you move them into storage. This guide will walk you through the process of using voice commands to track your seasonal gear, ensuring you always know the condition and location of your tires without ever needing to touch a keyboard.

The Quick Voice Command Method for Tires

Recording your winter tires in SmartStow is designed to be a hands-free experience. Because you must be online to add new items, ensure your mobile device has a data connection or Wi-Fi signal before beginning the process.

  • Step 1: Access the Recording Interface: Open the SmartStow app and tap the voice recording icon.
  • Step 2: State the Location First: Begin your sentence with the specific storage spot to help the system categorize the item. Example: "Garage Tire Rack..."
  • Step 3: Describe the Item: Speak clearly and include the brand and quantity. Example: "...Set of 4 Michelin Tires..."
  • Step 4: Add Technical Details: Mention the specific condition or tread depth to help with future maintenance. Example: "...6mm tread."
  • Step 5: Finalize the Entry: Once you stop speaking, SmartStow processes the voice-to-text data and saves the entry to your digital inventory.
