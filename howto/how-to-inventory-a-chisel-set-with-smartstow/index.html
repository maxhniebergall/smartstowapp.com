How to Inventory a Chisel Set with SmartStow?

January 30, 2026 • 5 min read

To inventory a chisel set, ensure you are online, open SmartStow, and speak the details clearly. State the location, quantity, brand, and specific type, such as "Garage, Woodworking, 4-piece Narex Richter bevel edge chisels." SmartStow records your voice to log the item for future offline access.

Maintaining an organized workshop is essential for any woodworker, but keeping track of high-quality hand tools like a chisel set can be a challenge. Traditional methods of inventory often fall short because they require manual data entry or complex setups that take time away from your craft. Whether you are a professional carpenter or a hobbyist, knowing exactly where your tools are and what condition they are in saves time and prevents duplicate purchases. SmartStow provides a modern solution by focusing on a voice-first approach that fits naturally into your workflow.

By using voice recording instead of manual typing, you can document your entire tool collection while your hands are still busy in the garage. This guide will walk you through the process of using SmartStow to log a premium chisel set, ensuring that every detail from the brand to the specific storage location is captured accurately. With the ability to search your inventory offline, you will never have to wonder where a specific tool is hidden again.

The Quick Voice Command Method

The most efficient way to build your inventory is by speaking the details directly into the app. This eliminates the need for tedious typing and allows you to stay focused on your organization tasks. Follow these steps to record your chisel set:

  • Check Connectivity: Ensure your mobile device is connected to the internet, as an active connection is required to record and add new items to your SmartStow database.
  • Open the App: Launch SmartStow on your device and navigate to the recording interface.
  • Initiate Voice Logging: Tap the microphone icon to begin your voice recording session.
  • Speak the Command: State the location first, followed by the specific item details. For example, say: "Garage, Woodworking, Tool Roll, 4-piece Narex Richter bevel edge chisels."
  • Review the Entry: The app will process your voice logging and create a text-based entry. Quickly verify that the brand name and quantities are correct.
  • Save and Sync: Once you confirm the details, the item is saved to your inventory and synced for future use.

Using voice commands ensures that you can log multiple tools in a fraction of the time it would take to write them down or enter them into a spreadsheet. This hands-free approach is particularly useful in a garage environment where your hands might be dusty or occupied with tools.
