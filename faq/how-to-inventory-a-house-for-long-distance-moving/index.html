Phase 1: The Pre-Inventory Strategy and Setup

Before you speak a single word into your device, you must establish a staging area. High-value moving requires a systematic approach where items are grouped by category and fragility. This preparation ensures that your voice recordings are logical and easy to search later.

  • Designate a 'High-Value Zone': Gather all jewelry, small electronics, and essential documents in one secure area.
  • Categorize by Room: Even if items are moving to a different room in the new house, inventory them based on their current location to maintain a clear trail.
  • Secure Packing Supplies: Ensure you have heavy-duty boxes and professional-grade padding ready before you start the recording process.
  • Establish a Numbering System: Use a bold marker to number every box clearly on at least three sides.
  • Check Connectivity: Ensure you are online when starting your session with SmartStow to allow the voice-to-text processing to function at peak performance.

The goal of this phase is to minimize the time spent thinking about where things go. When the inventory process begins, you should be able to move from one item to the next without hesitation. By using SmartStow, you are preparing a digital manifest that will be accessible even when you are offline at your new home, making the unpacking process significantly smoother.
